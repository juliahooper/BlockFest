Getting Started

Your First Steps

Blockchella runs on the Ethereum Sepolia testnet. You don't need real money — everything uses SETH (Sepolia ETH), which is free to obtain. Before you can buy a ticket or join a group, you need three things: a wallet, the right network, and some SETH.

Prerequisites

🦊
MetaMask Browser Extension
A browser-based Ethereum wallet. Available for Chrome, Firefox, Brave, and Edge.
🌐
Sepolia Testnet
Blockchella is deployed on Ethereum Sepolia — a test network where transactions use free test ETH instead of real money.
💧
Sepolia ETH (SETH)
Free test funds available from faucets. You need SETH to pay for tickets and gas fees.

Step 1 — Install MetaMask

Go to metamask.io ↗ and install the browser extension. Once installed, follow the setup wizard to create a new wallet. Write down your secret recovery phrase and store it somewhere safe — this is the only way to recover your wallet if you lose access.

⚠️ Never share your secret recovery phrase with anyone. Blockchella will never ask for it.

Step 2 — Switch to Sepolia

By default MetaMask connects to Ethereum Mainnet (real money). You need to switch to Sepolia testnet. Blockchella will attempt to switch you automatically when you connect, but you can also do it manually:

  1. Open MetaMask and click the network dropdown at the top
  2. Click Show test networks if Sepolia is not visible
  3. Select Sepolia test network

Step 3 — Get SETH from a Faucet

A faucet is a website that sends you free test ETH. Copy your MetaMask wallet address (click your account name at the top of MetaMask) and paste it into one of these:

Sepolia Faucet ↗ Alchemy Sepolia Faucet ↗ Infura Faucet ↗

It usually takes 30–60 seconds for SETH to arrive in your wallet. You only need a small amount — each ticket costs a fixed amount of SETH set at contract deployment, plus a tiny gas fee for the transaction.

Step 4 — Choose Your Role

Open the Blockchella app ↗ and pick your role on the landing page:

🎟 I'm Attending — Buy tickets, join groups, check your ticket status, return tickets.
🚪 I'm at the Door — Verify ticket holders by scanning their wallet address at the venue entrance.
🏟 I'm the Venue — Admin panel for the contract owner. Monitor sales, view all groups, withdraw funds.

Step 5 — Connect Your Wallet

On any attendee page, click Connect MetaMask. MetaMask will pop up and ask you to approve the connection. Once connected, the status bar at the top of the page will show your shortened wallet address and a green indicator.

If you're on the wrong network, Blockchella will automatically ask MetaMask to switch to Sepolia. Approve the network switch when prompted.

Common Setup Issues

"MetaMask not detected"
The MetaMask extension is not installed, or you're using a browser that doesn't support it. Install MetaMask from metamask.io and reload the page.
"Wrong network — please switch to Sepolia"
MetaMask is connected to a different network (e.g. Ethereum Mainnet). Click the network prompt or manually switch to Sepolia in MetaMask.
Transaction keeps failing
You may not have enough SETH to cover the ticket price plus gas. Get more SETH from a faucet and try again.
Page shows "Loading..." and never updates
The read-only RPC provider may be rate-limited. Refresh the page, or connect MetaMask to use your own provider.
