How it Works

Simple group ticketing in four steps

1

Create a Group

Create a group and add your friends wallet addresses. Set a deadline for everyone to purchase.

2

Share Your Group ID

Share your unique Group ID with your friends so they can join.

3

Everyone Buys Together

Each friend pays for their ticket. SETH is held in escrow until the whole group has paid.

4

Tickets Released Simultaneously

Once everyone pays the tickets are released to all group members at the same time. If anyone misses the deadline everyone gets refunded.
