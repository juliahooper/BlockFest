Blockchella Docs

Blockchella is a blockchain-based group ticketing system built on Ethereum Sepolia testnet. It uses a smart contract to coordinate group purchases, hold funds in escrow, and guarantee that either everyone in your group gets a ticket or no one is charged.

What is Blockchella?

Blockchella is a proof-of-concept event ticketing platform that replaces centralised ticket vendors with a transparent, trustless smart contract. Attendees form groups on-chain, pay into escrow together, and receive ERC-20 ticket tokens only when the full group has paid. If the group deadline passes without everyone paying, all escrow funds are automatically returned.

The system also includes a custom on-chain blockchain — a chain of blocks recorded inside the smart contract — that stores a permanent, cryptographically linked record of every completed ticket group.

Contract

The Blockchella smart contract is deployed on the Sepolia Ethereum testnet.
