Group Ticketing

How Group Ticketing Works

Blockchella's core feature is coordinated group purchasing. A group can have up to 4 members (including the creator). All members must pay before the deadline, or everyone gets their money back — no partial purchases, no partial refunds.

The Full Flow

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  1. Creator calls createGroup([friend1, friend2], 24) │
│     → Group #42 created, deadline in 24 hours        │
└─────────────────────────────┬───────────────────────┘
                              │
┌─────────────────────────────▼───────────────────────┐
│  2. Creator calls joinGroup(42)  { value: ticketPrice }│
│     → First payment received                         │
│     → 3 tickets RESERVED from available pool         │
│     → paidCount = 1 / 3                              │
└─────────────────────────────┬───────────────────────┘
                              │
┌─────────────────────────────▼───────────────────────┐
│  3. friend1 calls joinGroup(42)  { value: ticketPrice }│
│     → paidCount = 2 / 3                              │
└─────────────────────────────┬───────────────────────┘
                              │
┌─────────────────────────────▼───────────────────────┐
│  4. friend2 calls joinGroup(42)  { value: ticketPrice }│
│     → paidCount = 3 / 3  ← GROUP COMPLETE           │
│     → All 3 members receive 1 BFT ticket token       │
│     → Group recorded as block in chain               │
│     → SETH flows to contract (venue withdraws later) │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

The Escrow Mechanism

When a member pays, their SETH does not go directly to the venue. Instead, it is held in the contract's escrow — locked inside the smart contract and tracked per member. The venue cannot access it until the group completes and tickets are distributed.

This escrow guarantee means:

  • You cannot be charged without receiving a ticket
  • The venue cannot withdraw your money if the group fails
  • Refunds are automatic — no need to request them from a company

Ticket Reservation on First Payment

When the first member of a group pays, the contract immediately reserves one ticket for every member of the group from the available pool. This prevents a situation where your group is paying over time but tickets sell out underneath you.

The reserved tickets are not yours yet — they are held for the group. If the group fails, the reservation is released back to the pool and everyone is refunded.

💡 Implication: If only 50 tickets remain and your group has 4 members, those 4 tickets are locked for your group the moment the first person pays — even if the other 3 haven't paid yet.

All-or-Nothing Guarantee

This is the defining property of Blockchella group tickets. The contract enforces it at the code level — there is no way to partially complete a group purchase. Either all members pay before the deadline and everyone gets a ticket, or the deadline passes and everyone who paid is automatically refunded.

What Happens When the Deadline Expires

The group deadline is a Unix timestamp set when the group is created (e.g. 24 hours from creation). Once the deadline passes with an incomplete payment count:

  1. Anyone can call refundGroup(groupId) to trigger cleanup
  2. The group is marked Cancelled
  3. All members who paid have their SETH pushed back to their wallets
  4. Reserved tickets are released back to the available pool
  5. Members who had not paid owe nothing and remain unaffected
⚠️ Gas fees paid for the original transactions are not refunded — they are consumed by the Ethereum network regardless of the outcome.

Solo Purchase

Not everyone is going with friends. The Buy Solo tab on the Buy page lets you create a group with just yourself (no other members) and immediately purchase your ticket. Under the hood this still uses createGroup([], 1) followed by joinGroup — a group of one that completes instantly.

Group States

None The group ID does not exist. All fields are zero values.
Active The group exists and is accepting payments before its deadline.
Completed All members paid. Tickets distributed. Block added to chain.
Cancelled Deadline passed or creator cancelled. All escrow refunded.
